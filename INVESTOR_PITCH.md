# HealthBridge MedTrack

## Investor Pitch Deck

---

### The Problem

Zimbabwe's public health supply chain loses **34% of medicines** to expiry, theft, and misallocation. Stockouts at rural clinics force patients to travel hours for basic treatments. The Ministry of Health operates with **no real-time visibility** into what medicines exist, where they are, or when they'll expire — across 1,500+ facilities and 10 provinces.

**The cost is measured in lives.**

---

### The Solution

**HealthBridge MedTrack** is a real-time medicine traceability and intelligence platform that connects Zimbabwe's entire pharmaceutical supply chain — from NatPharm central warehouses to the smallest rural clinic dispensary.

We digitise the last mile of health delivery with:

| Module | Function |
|---|---|
| **National Stock Map** | Live geographic view of stock levels at every facility |
| **AI Reorder Engine** | Predicts stockouts 2 weeks in advance, auto-generates purchase requests |
| **e-GP Compliance** | Tracks QR-code scanning at receiving facilities (government procurement mandate) |
| **Consignment Tracking** | End-to-end visibility from dispatch to delivery |
| **Supplier Intelligence** | Performance scoring, on-time delivery, dispute tracking |
| **Impact & ROI Dashboard** | Quantified lives impacted, waste reduction, cost savings |
